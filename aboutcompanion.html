About MyOrtho Companion- Knee

Guiding patients confidently through every stage of knee replacement.

MyOrtho Companion began after watching my father-in-law struggle through a knee replacement recovery that was confusing, fragmented, and ultimately led to a reoperation. He didn’t know what to expect, got conflicting advice from his care team, and became discouraged. I built this app for people like him—so no one feels lost during recovery again.

Why We Exist

Many knee replacement patients face uncertainty, conflicting guidance, and lack of motivation. We created MyOrtho Companion to make recovery clear, consistent, and empowering—bridging the gap between the surgeon, the therapist, and the patient.

The Journey

1. Pre-Treatment Tracking

Patients log symptoms, treatments, and pain history to build a clear picture of their knee health and treatment path—helping them and their surgeon decide the right time for surgery.

2. Preparing for Surgery

We emphasize expectation setting, education, and mental readiness. Journaling and self-reflection tools help patients feel more in control and reduce anxiety about what’s ahead.

3. Recovery and Rehab

The Activity Dashboard encourages daily movement—not just formal PT. Patients track small wins like walking to the mailbox or cooking dinner, and unlock motivational achievements to stay engaged.

What Makes It Different

This isn’t just another health tracker. It’s a full companion—from your first step on the treatment ladder to your final milestone in recovery. The app builds confidence through simplicity, motivation through structure, and results through consistency.

Smart Features That Matter:

  • Pre-op journaling and treatment ladder visualization
  • Educational content that’s actually understandable
  • Daily rehab tracking and activity rewards
  • Shareable progress data with your care team

Our Mission

Recovery isn’t just about physical therapy—it’s about mindset, expectations, and everyday life. We’re here to make that journey easier, clearer, and more successful for every patient who opens the app.
